Welcome to Care!

New and dynamic, our medical and paramedical rehabilitation centre caters for our patients’ health and well-being in many different ways.

Our team of passionate therapists is ready to assist you every step of the way to your recovery. Our brand new premises are especially equipped to help us meet your needs, thereby providing the upmost professional medical focus. We are committed to using all the resources available to us in the hope of making your pain less of a burden. Every single member of our team is here to help and guide you towards a “better you”.

Our aim? Always innovate, so as to ensure patients’ well-being. We strive to keep ourselves updated on the latest techniques and technology to make lives better; we are here to listen and address everybody’s concerns to the very best of our abilities. We also believe team work is, and always will be, the best way to help people!

Our Services

General Practice (GP)

The GP is a specialisat trained to work in the front line of a healthcare system and to take the initial steps to provide care for any health problem(s) that patients may have.

READ MORE

Physiotherapy

Highly qualified and professional, physiotherapists work together with their patients to help them get better and to prevent relapse in the long-term.

READ MORE

Osteopathy

Offering an innovative approach to health care, osteopaths are professionals who propose a functional improvement technique that recognises the important link between the structure of the body and its functional movement.

READ MORE

Massage Therapy

Assisting patients on both the physical and mental fronts, massage therapists have been relieving people of pain for years.

READ MORE

Podiatry

The Podiatrist offers comprehensive general foot care including treatment of nails, corns, callus, bunions, warts, cracked heels, and dry skin. The service has a specific focus on the foot care and maintenance of people with diabetes and uses the latest in diagnostic and therapeutic equipment, dressings and advice.

READ MORE

Exercise Physiology

CARE Exercise Physiologists have the clinical knowledge, experience and skills needed to deliver holistic, patient-centred exercise interventions.  Scientific research, validated assessment procedures, clinical judgement and patient goals are used to evaluate, deliver and monitor your exercise and rehabilitation program.  We will empower you to regain your health!

READ MORE

 

Psycotherapy

Psychotherapy is the support, by a person specially trained for this purpose, of a person who is suffering or in a state of internal disagreement that may be manifested by stress, behavioral problems or a state of distress.
